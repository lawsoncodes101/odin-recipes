Creamy, cheesy, smoky, rich Carbonara.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pancetta (or bacon)
  • Garlic
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  • Cook the Spaghetti
  • Fry the Pancetta
  • Prepare Egg-Cheese Mixture
  • Combine Pasta and Pancetta
  • Mix in Egg-Cheese Sauce
  • Season and Serve
